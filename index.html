Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Overview

StreetChef ("we", "our", or "the app") is a food truck management application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

StreetChef is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No third-party services collect your information

What Data the App Stores Locally

The app stores the following information only on your device:

  • Orders and event details
  • Client information (names, contacts)
  • Menu items and ingredients
  • Work session records
  • Gas cylinder and equipment tracking
  • Checklists and notes

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. All information remains on your device.

Data Export

You can export your data at any time using the backup feature within the app. This creates a local file that you control.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual records within the app
  • Uninstalling the application

Children's Privacy

StreetChef is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
